Mr.Robot

The series tell the story of a young man who suffers from social anxiety disorder and is dissatisfied with society. Working as a computer programmer in the morning and a hacker in the evening,he is assigned to work by a mysterious man who call him self "Mr.robot" to infiltrate large corporation they believe are corrupting society.
