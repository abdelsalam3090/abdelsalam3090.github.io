Overview

Harry Potter 20th Anniversary: Returning to Hogwarts is the 2022 special reunion film from the Harry Potter series that is released on HBO Max on New Year's Day. It marks the 20th anniversary of the release of the first installment of the series, Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (2001). whereas cast members from all of the "Harry Potter" films gather for a special retrospective to celebrate the anniversary of the first film, including interviews and talks.
